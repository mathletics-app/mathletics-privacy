Request Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Mathletics account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Deletion:

  1. Send an email to mathleticscare@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete My Mathletics Account".
  2. Include the email address you used to register in the app.
  3. Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 7 days of the request.

Data Deleted:

  • Your authentication account (email/password).
  • All practice data, stats, and quiz results stored in Firestore.
  • Premium subscription records (if any).

Data Retained:

None. Once deletion is confirmed, all user-specific data is permanently removed. No backups are retained.
